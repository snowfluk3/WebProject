About Us

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Vancouver's historic Gastown, what started as a vision of community and caffeine between friends, has become so much more. The Brew on Cordova has become a cozy space for students, artists, and locals to share ideas over handcrafted coffee. Our shop was built on the idea that good coffee brings people together. Our values keep us grounded, from the beans we choose to the atmosphere we create.

Our mission is simple: serve great coffee, support local roasters, and create a place where everyone feels at home.

Meet the Team

Grayson – Head Barista


Grayson crafts every espresso with passion and creativity. When he's not dancing around behind the bar, he's pretending he's a DJ, finding the perfect music for every occaision on the sound system.

Luke - Owner


Luke has a keen eye for design and business management. Every part of the cafe fits in his mind like a puzzle piece, until it all comes together in his artistic vision.

Victor - Head Chef


Victor is always hard at work in the kitchens in the backroom, either cooking up sandwiches or baking biscuits. Just holler: "Here Chef!" and he'll come running. Like any proper chef, he may meet any request with swearing, but he'll get it done with skill, finnesse, and the smallest smidge of panache.

Austen - Vibe Specialist


Austen's always around spreading good vibes! If you have any odd orders he's your guy, and you can trust he'll answer you bluntly and honestly.
